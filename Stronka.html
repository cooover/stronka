Tesla Office Building

WHAT?

The design for the office building located at Foksal in the center of Warsaw. The choice of the company arised from the ecological aspect of the company activity.

HOW?

The design for Tesla company calls for exhibition space which is located at the ground floor. The place is designed for the latest models of Tesla cars and the history of the company presentation. At the ground floor there is also a conference room. The other three floors are used for office space for 150 employees. Materials that were used in the design are a combination of architectural concrete and glulam frame. Photovoltaic panels are movable part of the elevation.

WHY?

Shop windows with the display of the latest models of cars ideally blend with the prestigious surroundings of the Foksal Street. The wooden construction (that emphasizes the ecological nature of the company) combine with raw, concrete lump give the building a modern look whereas the division of the elevation refers to classic proportions of adjacent tenements. Movable photovoltaic panels located at the south side of the building are controlled to use sunlight as much as it is possible and shield the inside of the building from excessive light at the same time..
